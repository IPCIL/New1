I am a Research Assistant Professor at the New York University Electrical and Computer Engineering department. I am also a member of NYU Wireless. I was employed as a lecturer and a postdoctoral researcher at the University of Michigan in 2017. I received my Ph.D. in Electrical Engineering at the University of Michigan in April 2017. My Ph.D. advisor was Sandeep Pradhan. I also received my M.Sc. in Applied Mathematics in 2016. My research interests are in the general areas of privacy and security, communications, information theory, and learning theory.
